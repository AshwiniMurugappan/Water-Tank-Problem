How it Works

The Water Tank Problem calculates how much rainwater can be trapped between blocks of varying heights. Using the O(n) two-pointer algorithm, we find the water level at each position by tracking the maximum heights from both left and right sides.

Example: Try 0,4,0,0,0,6,0,6,4,0 to see 18 units of water trapped.
